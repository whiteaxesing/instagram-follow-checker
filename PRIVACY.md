# Privacy Policy — Insta Follow Checker

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Data Collection

Insta Follow Checker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## How it works

The extension reads your existing Instagram session cookies (only accessible within the instagram.com domain) to make API requests on your behalf. These requests are made directly from your browser to Instagram's servers — no data passes through any third-party server.

## Data not collected

- No personal information
- No authentication credentials
- No browsing history
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports

## Third parties

No data is sold, transferred, or shared with any third party.
